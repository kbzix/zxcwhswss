Instructions and Overview

Micro Manager, in his desperation to gain the power of the golden apples has begun to take control of planet Appelmoeshapje. As Micro Manager's influence grows, Appel finds not only the inhabitants of, but the very world is turning against him! - Help Appel to navigate through each level, avoiding danger, collecting as many golden apples as you can before finally facing Micro Manager and restoring peace to the world.
